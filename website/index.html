PRIVACY & SAFETY

Your health data stays yours.

Encrypted at rest

All health data is encrypted in storage. Firebase-backed with strict per-user access rules.

No PII to AI

AI prompts are sanitized and anonymized — your identity is never sent to the model.

Never a diagnosis

The coach hedges with care ("consider", "you may want to") — not medical advice.

Delete anything, anytime

Account deletion is a full cascade across Firestore, Storage, and Auth. Truly gone.
