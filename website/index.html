FAQ

Quick answers.

Is Fitup free?+
Yes — the full app is free to use. A Pro tier is coming with advanced AI plans and unlimited insights. The web app is open right now at no cost.
Does it work without GPS or Health Connect?+
Absolutely. GPS and Health Connect are mobile bonuses — every other module (diet, workout, vitals, mental, community, AI coach) works fully on web.
Is the AI giving medical advice?+
No. The coach is built with explicit hedging — it suggests, never prescribes. For diagnosis or treatment, always talk to a clinician.
What about my data?+
Encrypted at rest. Anonymized before any AI call. You can delete your account and everything goes — Firestore, Storage, Auth — in one cascade.
What platforms is Fitup on?+
Android and Web are live right now. iOS is feature-complete and in launch hardening — App Store submission is next.
