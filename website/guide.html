MODULE · ACTIVITY

Track Activity

Capture every step, run, walk, jog, or cycle — with GPS map, live pace, and calorie burn. Sleep tracking included.

Live activity logging (mobile)

  1. Tap Activity from the sidebar or home card.
  2. Hit the big + button → choose Run, Walk, Jog, or Cycle.
  3. Permission prompt for location → tap Allow.
  4. Tap Start — your route, pace, distance, and calories track live with a map underneath.
  5. Tap Finish when done. You'll see the summary card with the route map, total stats, and earned Fitcoins.

Daily / weekly view (any platform)

The Activity page shows today's steps + calories + distance, your weekly trend, and a list of recent sessions. Tap any session to open its summary with the route.

Sleep

From the Activity page, tap Log Sleep to enter bedtime + wake time. The AI uses this to read recovery and adjust workout intensity.

Web users: live GPS isn't available — but everything you log on mobile syncs in instantly. Recent activity summary, sleep log, and AI insights all work on web.

MODULE · DIET

Log Diet & Water

Three ways to log a meal — pick the fastest one. Global and Indian cuisine, plus packaged food.

Log a meal

  1. Open Diet. You'll see today's calorie ring and meal slots (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks).
  2. Tap a meal slot or the + Log Meal button.
  3. Choose your method:
    • Search — type "rajma chawal" or "chicken caesar", pick portion.
    • Photo — snap a picture; AI identifies the dish and macros.
    • Barcode — scan packaged food.
  4. Tap Save. The calorie ring and macro bars update immediately.

Water logging

Tap the water glass on the Diet page. Use + / − to adjust glasses. The bar fills toward your daily goal.

Edit anything

Tap any saved meal slot to see the Edit pill — change portions, add items, or remove anything. All meals saved that day come up pre-filled.

💡 Power move: Tap Diet AI at the top to ask "what should I eat for dinner tonight?" — the AI uses your activity, vitals, and remaining macros to answer.

MODULE · WORKOUT

Do a Workout

AI-built strength + cardio plans tuned to your equipment and goals. Each exercise has video guidance.

Generate your first plan

  1. Open Workout. Tap Generate Plan.
  2. Pick your goal — fat loss, muscle gain, endurance, or general fitness.
  3. Select level — beginner, intermediate, expert.
  4. Pick environment — home, gym, outdoor.
  5. Tick what equipment you have access to.
  6. Tap Build my plan. AI returns a weekly schedule.

Do a session

  1. Tap today's session card on the Workout page.
  2. You'll see the exercise list with sets/reps and a video preview on each.
  3. Tap any exercise → log the actual sets/reps/weight as you do them.
  4. When done, tap Finish Session — calorie burn, time, and Fitcoins drop in.
The plan adapts weekly based on how you actually trained, slept, and ate. Skipped Monday's session? AI rebalances the rest of the week.

MODULE · HEALTH & VITALS

Upload Lab Reports

Snap your blood test PDF or image — the AI extracts every vital, fills in the trends, and flags what needs attention.

Lab report scan — 4 taps

  1. Open Health & Vitals. Tap Scan Lab Report.
  2. Pick a PDF or take a photo of the report.
  3. Tap AI Detect — the AI extracts vitals like HbA1c, Cholesterol, Vitamin D, Uric Acid, Liver, Thyroid (20+ types). Review the list — uncheck anything wrong.
  4. Tap Save Data. Every vital lands on the Health page with a status dot (good / moderate / needs attention).

Log a vital manually

Tap the + Log Vital button on Health page. Pick the vital, enter the number + unit. Done.

See trends

Tap any vital tile to open its trend chart over time. The AI insight below explains why a number is changing and what to do about it.

Connected devices

On mobile, Fitup reads vitals from Health Connect (Android) or HealthKit (iOS) — heart rate, blood oxygen, more — automatically.

⚠️ Not medical advice. The AI suggests with words like "consider" or "you may want to". For diagnosis or treatment, talk to a doctor.

MODULE · MENTAL WELLBEING

Mental Wellbeing

Mood tracking, stress surveys, guided meditation, and breathing exercises — all with weekly AI insight.

Daily mood check-in

  1. Open Mental Wellbeing. The mood emoji row is at the top.
  2. Tap the emoji that matches how you feel right now.
  3. (Optional) Add a one-line note — what's going on.
  4. That's it. The weekly mood graph updates.

Weekly stress survey

Every Sunday, you'll get a quick 6-question survey. Answers feed into your stress score, which the AI cross-references against sleep, activity, and vitals.

Meditation & breathing

  1. Tap Meditate on the Mental page.
  2. Pick a sound (rain, ocean, white noise…) and duration (5 / 10 / 15 / 20 min).
  3. Tap Start. Or pick Breathing for a 4-7-8 guided exercise.
The weekly insight ties everything together — e.g. "Your best mood days followed walks after 7pm. Want me to build that into your plan?"

THE BIG ONE

Get a Holistic AI Health Plan

This is where Fitup pulls everything together. One plan — activity + diet + workout + recovery — that knows your vitals, mood, and goals.

①

Open Home → Holistic Plan

On the Home page, scroll to the Holistic Plan card. If you don't have an active plan yet, tap Generate Plan.

②

Tell the AI about your life

Quick multi-step form:

  • Which modules you want included (activity, diet, workout, mental…)
  • Availability — low / medium / high time per week
  • Intensity preference — easy / balanced / push
  • Start date and goal target
  • Any specific intents ("lose 5 kg", "lower cholesterol", "sleep better")
③

AI reads your full picture

It pulls your latest vitals, recent activity, eating patterns, sleep, mood and stress, and your goal — then generates a connected plan in 5–10 seconds.

④

Your plan, broken into 4 lanes

Activity
Step targets, weekly walks/runs/cycles
Diet
Daily calorie/macro/water targets aligned to your activity
Workout
Weekly strength / cardio split tuned to recovery
Recovery
Sleep window, mindfulness, breathing cadence
⑤

Track and adjust

Every module's page shows how it's tracking against the plan. The AI re-reads weekly and can amend the plan if your reality shifts — illness, travel, missed sessions, new lab results.

⑥

Ask the coach anything

Tap any module's AI button — Move AI, Diet AI, Burn AI, Health AI, Wellbeing AI — to ask specific questions like "should I run today?" or "is my Vitamin D bad?".

REAL EXAMPLE OF HOLISTIC AI
You upload a lab report showing high uric acid. You've been running 15 km/day. Your sleep is averaging 5h. A normal app would let it continue.

Fitup's coach reads all three — your lab, your activity, your sleep — and flags the conflict immediately. It pulls back the running target, swaps in low-impact mobility, raises your water goal, adjusts your dinner toward purine-low foods, and pushes your bedtime earlier. One adjustment across 4 modules in one tap.
